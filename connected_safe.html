Introduction

In this project, the objective was to use our new knowledge acquired during the 4 previous clouds, i.e. the Arduino with components, UDP to make the Arduino cards communicate with the Pcs and Python. The aim was to create an Arduino system that would represent something like a weather station, a safe or a bar, all of which would be connected to some form of automation (home automation) using UDP and Python.
